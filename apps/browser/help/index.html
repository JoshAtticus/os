Browser Help

Browser Alpha v1.0.3 for AtticusOS | CORS is enabled (some sites might not load properly)

Browser is the iFrame-based internet browser for AtticusOS. It works by embeddding the website you request in an iFrame.

CORS

CORS is a known issue with this browser. If you're looking for workarounds, type corsinfo into the URL bar and press go.

Are you a developer trying to get your website to work in this browser? Make sure you allow iFrames in your CORS poilicy.

Become a partner!

If you're a developer who has made your site accessible using this browser, send an email to joshatticus02 at gmail dot com.

From there, your site may appear as an alternative to incompatible sites, or be featured on the new tab page.
