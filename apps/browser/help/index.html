Browser Help

Browser v2.0 for AtticusOS

Browser is the iFrame-based internet browser for AtticusOS. It works by embedding the website you request in an iFrame.

Become a partner!

If you're a developer who has made your site accessible using this browser, send an email to joshatticus02 at gmail dot com.

From there, your site may appear as an alternative to incompatible sites, or be featured on the new tab page.
